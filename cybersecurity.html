Cybersecurity:

Cybersecurity focuses on protecting digital systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. It encompasses technologies, processes, and practices aimed at safeguarding information assets and ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data. Effective cybersecurity involves risk management, threat detection and prevention, incident response, and ongoing education and awareness efforts. It plays a critical role in safeguarding individuals, organizations, and nations against cyber threats in our increasingly interconnected world.
